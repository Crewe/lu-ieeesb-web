Events

Keeping In Touch

The Lakehead IEEE student branch has started up some exciting new ways for you to keep track of what's going on.

Google Calendar

The Lakehead IEEE Student Branch Calendar is being maintained to keep you up-to-date on all of the latest events being planned. It also has ICAL format if you want that.

Twitter

The Lakehead IEEE Student Branch Twitter Account provides a real-time look at what's happening in the branch office right now. We even have a motion detector set up that shows whether people are in the office or not!

Tumblr

The Lakehead IEEE Student Branch Tumblr Blog is essentially a newsletter for members. It copies to Twitter.

RSS Feeds

The Twitter, Tumblr and Google Calendar tubes have RSS feed <links> in the site. Most browsers show a handy orange RSS icon to see these.

Moral of the story: The internet is a series of tubes.

Tutorials

Many student-run tutorials have been conducted in the branch office in the past, with topics including Atmel microcontrollers, C/C++, and Matlab. If you have a suggestion for a tutorial, we encourage you to bring it up.

Lectures and Guest Speakers

If you have any ideas for a guest speaker presentation or if you wish to conduct a presentation yourself, bring the idea forward.

Competitions

IEEE Awards, Competitions and Scholarships

The IEEE hosts dozens of competitions, awards and scholarships for both individual and team-based activities. If you see one for which you feel up to the challenge, say so!
